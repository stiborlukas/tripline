Gain/loss is computed from your file's own GPS/barometer elevation, lightly filtered for sensor noise. Strava, Garmin, and Komoot each run their own proprietary elevation-correction model against their own terrain data, so their numbers will still differ from this — and from each other — even on the exact same ride. That's normal, not a bug.
